Mango Mint Mocktail

Description

This refreshing tropical twist on a mint mojito is the perfect drink to whip up for a dinner party! It always impresses with little effort and the flavor of the mango compliments the citrus and mint so well. The mocktail is truly a mango lover's dream!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mango puree
  • handful of mint leaves
  • 2 cups club soda or seltzer water
  • 1/3 cup simple syrup
  • lemon wedges
  • lime wedges
  • ice

Instructions

  1. To a food processor, add fresh mango and blend until a smooth puree forms.

  2. Add 2 to 3 mint leaves into each cup along with a lemon wedge and lime wedge. Use a muddler to crush the juice out of the citrus fruits and release the flavors of the mint

  3. Then add about 1/4 cup of mango puree to each cup, adding less or more depending on your tastebuds.

  4. Add about 1 to 2 tablespoons of simple syrup to each cup depending on how sweet you like it

  5. Fill the rest of the cup with ice and club soda.Stir to mix all the flavors together and enjoy!
